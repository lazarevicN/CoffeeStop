About

Before reaching your table, our coffee goes a long way. From plantations in the farthest corners of the globe, through the hands of farmers and fruit-pickers, to the roasting and finest grinding in our mills, and yet, every cup means just one fresh start to enjoying the day ahead.

Although a very young brand, in less than three years proven quality and constant innovation, we have become one of the fastest growing cafeteria chains in Serbia, which in its range currently offers more than 15 unique types of coffee from all over the world, among which are very rare flavors, exclusive to this part of Europe.

Cafeteria guests can wake up each morning to the flavors of India or Brazil espresso, enhance productivity with the flavors of Colombia, Kenya or Ethiopia, enjoy specially prepared blends or end the day with decaffeinated beverages.

Right Coffee - The Key To The Best Taste!

What makes our offer special is the fact that the process of roasting and then grinding takes place just before consumption, and quantities are always dosed, so that coffee that has traveled even from India, Kenya, Colombia or Brazil always has a fresh and distinctive taste .
